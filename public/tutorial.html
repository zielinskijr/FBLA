how does this game work?

your main goals

you have three main goals in this game!

  • keep your pet alive for seven days or longer.

  • make sure your pet is taken care of. make it happy, make sure it's not too tired, and make sure it's not hungry.

  • in the process of doing that, you need to keep your budget up. try and earn as much money as possible while still keeping your pet alive. don't go in debt, otherwise, the pet will be repossesed.

how do i take care of my pet?

simple!

keep an eye out on the stats. you will get a warning on the screen reminding you to take care of your pet. yellow warnings are not bad, red means your pet will die soon.

each stat has a respective button. if the pet is hungry, feed it. if the pet is sick, send it to the hospital, if the pet is tired, send it to bed, etc.

how do i earn money?

earning money is important! in order to feed your dog, you need money, to play, you need money, etc.

currently, there are two ways to earn money.

  • you earn 5$ at the end of every day. this isn't enough to keep you afloat, but it's a small reward for making it to a new day.

  • you can earn a random amount of money between 50-100$ by doing a pet show. this does drain your pet's stats, so do with with care.
